Photographic

Fotogenic is an app that allows its users to easily create detailed, intuitive, and accurate reminders with photos.

• Keep updated on tasks and interests • Schedule reminders to never forget important tasks • Use photos to capture key details quickly • Further enhance reminders with annotations on the photos

Using the idea that a photo is worth a thousand words, Fotogenic aims to help organize even the smallest details of our lives. Fotogenic utilizes the efficiency and simplistic intuition necessary to take photos, and returns a clear, flexible, and tailored reminder. It's fast, it's detailed, it's Fotographic.
